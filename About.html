  • My Why:
    I've always dreamed of becoming a school librarian, but it didn’t seem like a realistic career path. In 2015, after being diagnosed with cancer, I made a promise to myself: once I healed and the kids were older, I would pursue my dream to be a school librarian. I will graduate with my School Library and Media master's degree this summer and then I plan on pursuing a career as a school librarian.
  • My Career:
    I’ve worked as an LBD Special Education teacher in elementary schools for several years. Before that, I taught preschool and Kindergarten in both public and private settings.
  • My Educational Background:
    • EKU: Child and Family Studies Bachelors degree
    • Georgetown College: LBD Education Masters degree
    • EKU: School Library and Media Science Masters degree (currently pursuing)

Where I am on the Map

I live in work in Claryville, KY. Claryville is an unincorporated suburb in Southern Campbell County with a population of a around 3,000 people. It has beautiful parks and a highly rated elementary school. Claryville was absorbed into ALexandria around the year 1919.
